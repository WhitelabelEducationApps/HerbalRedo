Privacy Policy — Effective April 27, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how WhitelabelEducationApps handles information in connection with the Herbal - Medicinal Plants Android app.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data:

  • No account or login required.
  • No analytics, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs.
  • No advertisements.
  • No user data sent to any server.

3. Permissions Explained

  • INTERNET & ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — Required for Google Maps tiles.
  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION & ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION — On-device only for map display. Never transmitted or stored.
  • SET_WALLPAPER — Lets you set plant images as wallpaper. No data leaves your device.

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses Google Maps. Please review the Google Privacy Policy.
